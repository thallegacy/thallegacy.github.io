Lynell Robinson III Bio

Lynell Robinson is a Project Management Consultant with the Newark Alliance. In this role, Lynell supports and drives the operational success of the Newark 2020 hiring initiative that empowers Newark residents through economic inclusion and expanding opportunities to connect to jobs in local corporations and institutions.

He received his B.S. in Computer Science from Morehouse in 2007 and holds various certifications including a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt from the University of St Thomas

Lynell is no stranger to business process management, having spent several years as a Business Process Analyst where he used process innovation, industry best practices and training strengths to establish operational excellence within an organization. Before joining the Newark Alliance in 2018, Lynell worked for 10 years for a diverse range of Minnesota-based Fortune 100 organizations, including Target Corporation and UnitedHealth Group.
